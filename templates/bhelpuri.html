Delicious Bhelpuri

Ingredients

  • 3 cups puffed rice (murmura)
  • 1 cup sev (crispy gram flour noodles)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup boiled potato, diced
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 2 tbsp tamarind chutney
  • 2 tbsp mint-coriander chutney
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the puffed rice and sev.
  2. Add the chopped onions, tomatoes, potatoes, peanuts, and coriander leaves to the bowl.
  3. Sprinkle chaat masala, cumin powder, and salt over the mixture.
  4. Add the tamarind chutney and mint-coriander chutney to the bowl.
  5. Squeeze lemon juice over the mixture.
  6. Gently mix all the ingredients together, ensuring everything is well combined but not crushed.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  8. Serve immediately in individual portions or small paper cones for an authentic street food experience.

Serving Suggestion

Serve Bhelpuri immediately after mixing to maintain its crunchiness. It's best enjoyed as a light snack or appetizer. For an extra touch of authenticity, serve in small paper cones or bowls made from dried leaves.

Pair it with a cold glass of sweet lime soda or masala chai for a perfect Indian street food experience!
