Delicious Paneer Pakora

Ingredients

For the pakora:

  • 250g paneer, cut into 2-inch rectangles
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup water (approx.)
  • Oil for deep frying

For the garnish:

  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Chaat masala for sprinkling

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix gram flour, rice flour, red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt.
  2. Gradually add water to the flour mixture and whisk to form a smooth, thick batter. The consistency should be such that it coats the back of a spoon.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadai over medium heat.
  4. Dip each paneer piece in the batter, ensuring it's evenly coated.
  5. Carefully slide the batter-coated paneer into the hot oil.
  6. Fry the pakoras until they turn golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  7. Remove the pakoras with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  8. Repeat the process with the remaining paneer pieces.
  9. In a serving plate, arrange the hot paneer pakoras.
  10. Garnish with sliced onions, chopped green chilies, and fresh coriander leaves.
  11. Sprinkle some chaat masala over the pakoras for extra flavor.

Serving Suggestion

Serve these crispy and flavorful paneer pakoras hot with green chutney or sweet tamarind chutney. They make a perfect snack for rainy days, as an appetizer for your Indian-themed dinner party, or as a delicious teatime treat!
