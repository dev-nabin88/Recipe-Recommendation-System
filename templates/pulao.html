Delicious Veg Pulao

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice, rinsed and soaked for 30 minutes
  • 3 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, cauliflower)
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 green cardamom pods
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup cashews, roasted (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee or oil in a large pot or pressure cooker over medium heat.
  2. Add cinnamon stick, bay leaves, cardamom pods, cloves, and cumin seeds. Sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add mixed vegetables, mint leaves, and coriander leaves. Sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add turmeric powder and salt. Mix well.
  7. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot. Gently stir to coat the rice with the spices and vegetables.
  8. Pour in water or vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
  9. Cover and cook for about 15-18 minutes or until the rice is tender and all the liquid is absorbed.
  10. Once done, let it rest for 5 minutes, then fluff the rice gently with a fork.
  11. Sprinkle lemon juice over the pulao and mix lightly.
  12. Garnish with roasted cashews before serving.

Serving Suggestion

Serve hot Veg Pulao with raita (yogurt side dish) or a simple cucumber salad. It pairs well with papadum or a side of pickle. For a complete meal, you can serve it with a dal (lentil curry) or a vegetable curry of your choice. This aromatic and flavorful rice dish is perfect for both everyday meals and special occasions.
