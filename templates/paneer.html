Delicious Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

For the paneer:

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 1 tbsp butter

For the curry:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 4 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves, chopped

Instructions

  1. In a pan, melt 1 tbsp butter and lightly fry the paneer cubes until golden. Set aside.
  2. In a large pot, melt 2 tbsp butter over medium heat. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add pureed tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and cumin powder. Cook for 10-15 minutes until the oil separates from the masala.
  5. Reduce heat and add heavy cream. Stir well and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Add the fried paneer cubes to the curry and gently stir to coat them with the sauce.
  7. Crush the kasuri methi between your palms and sprinkle it over the curry. Add salt to taste.
  8. Simmer for another 5 minutes to let the flavors meld together.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.

Serving Suggestion

Serve hot Paneer Butter Masala with naan bread, roti, or steamed basmati rice. For an extra touch of richness, drizzle a little cream on top just before serving. This creamy and flavorful dish pairs well with a side of cucumber raita or a fresh green salad.
