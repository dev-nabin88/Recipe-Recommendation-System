Creamy Lassi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups plain yogurt (curd)
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 2-3 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • A pinch of saffron strands (optional)
  • 1 tbsp rose water (optional)
  • Ice cubes
  • 2 tbsp chopped pistachios or almonds for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a blender, add yogurt, water, sugar, and cardamom powder.
  2. Blend the mixture until smooth and frothy.
  3. If using saffron, soak a few strands in 1 tablespoon of warm milk for 5 minutes, then add to the blender.
  4. Add rose water if desired and pulse to combine.
  5. Taste and adjust sweetness if needed.
  6. Fill glasses with ice cubes.
  7. Pour the Lassi over the ice cubes.
  8. Garnish with chopped pistachios or almonds.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing Lassi!

Serving Suggestion

Serve Lassi chilled as a refreshing drink on hot summer days or as a complement to spicy Indian meals. It's perfect for cooling down and aiding digestion. For a fancy presentation, you can rim the glasses with a mixture of sugar and cardamom powder.

Pro Tips:

  • For a thicker Lassi, use less water or add some cream.
  • You can create variations by adding fruits like mango or strawberry for flavored Lassi.
  • For a savory version, omit the sugar and add a pinch of roasted cumin powder and black salt.
  • Chill the glasses before serving for an extra cool drink.
