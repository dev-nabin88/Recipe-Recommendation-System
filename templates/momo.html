Delicious Steam Momos

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup water

For the filling:

  • 1 cup minced vegetables (cabbage, carrots, onions)
  • 1/2 cup minced paneer or tofu
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Gradually add water and knead into a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, mix all the filling ingredients together.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each into a thin circle.
  4. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each circle.
  5. Fold and pleat the edges to seal the momo, creating a dumpling shape.
  6. Prepare a steamer by lining it with cabbage leaves or parchment paper.
  7. Arrange the momos in the steamer, leaving space between each.
  8. Steam for 10-12 minutes or until the dough becomes translucent.
  9. Serve hot with a dipping sauce of your choice.
